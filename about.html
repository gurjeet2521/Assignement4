HARRY ROSEN

About Us

Harry Rosen is one of the best high-end men’s clothing speciality retailers in the world. As Canadians we’re uncomfortable boasting, but this is the opinion we hear from business travellers, tourists, even our suppliers, and above all, from our customers – sophisticated men who shop for men’s fashion all over the world. We are a proudly Canadian company with 17 stores in eight major Canadian markets, employing over 1,000 associates.

We work as hard as we can to make sure that all of our customers have an enjoyable and memorable experience at Harry Rosen. At the heart of it all is our personal approach to service, something that has been the guiding principle of our company since Harry Rosen and his brother Lou opened the first store in Cabbagetown, Toronto, over 60 years ago. Our role as a clothing advisor is to assist and guide men towards the clothing that helps best represent them. Over time, we build a bond of trust with each client, helping him develop his personal style, advising him on additions to his wardrobe, making sure he can be confident that he is properly attired for every circumstance and occasion. Essentially, your associate acts as your personal shopper, keeping a file of your preferences and your needs. He or she can send you pictures of new arrivals, arrange private shopping appointments and help speed up the process by assembling selections from which you can choose.

We are your source for the world’s leading menswear designers including Armani Collezioni, Canali, Ermenegildo Zegna, Hugo Boss, Burberry, Polo Ralph Lauren, Canada Goose, Brunello Cucinelli, Dolce and Gabbana, Loro Piana, Moncler, Parajumpers, John Varvatos, Tom Ford, and many more. We also carry footwear from Prada, Salvatore Ferragamo, Tod’s, John Varvatos, Giuseppe Zanotti, Lanvin, Maison Margiela, and more.We also offer Made to Measure tailoring from a number of designer collections as well as the ultimate expression of the tailor’s art – our own Bespoke tailoring. From formal wear to jeans, sportswear to suits and sports jackets, rugged outerwear parkas to superbly hand-crafted dress shoes, we strive to dress each of our customers appropriately for the occasion.

Our follow-up service is also unparalleled and includes Complimentary Alterations on regularly priced merchandise, our Lifetime Maintenance Guarantee and a generous Return Policy, all designed to make you look and feel your best. Many of our associates speak more than one language and we can serve you in the language of your choice – Mandarin, Cantonese, Persian, Greek, Italian, French, German, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Arabic, etcetera. Please ask us how we can assist you in your clothing needs.
